Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1/4 cup of butter

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, cook the bacon until crispy.
  2. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  4. Place the bread butter side down in the skillet, and add a slice of cheddar cheese and a few slices of tomato to each sandwich.
  5. Top with another slice of bread, butter side up.
  6. Cook the sandwiches until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  7. Serve the sandwiches warm, topped with the cooked bacon.
